Spicy Plum Marinade

Do you love meat combined with sweet sauces? Then you have to try the marinade from plum and orange marmalade. There is also chili pepper in it, which gives it a pleasant taste tip. Start grilling and impress your friends with new flavors.


  • Preparation time: 5 minutes
  • Difficulty: easy

Ingredients

(1 serving)
  • 12 pieces of plum or jam
  • 1 cup of orange juice
  • 1 red chili pepper
  • 1/2 cup of orange marmalade

Procedure

1

If we have fresh plums, finely chop them.

2

Crush the pepper.

3

Put everything in a pot, add orange juice and marmalade and cook slowly for about half an hour.

4

Brush the meat with the marinade while grilling.
